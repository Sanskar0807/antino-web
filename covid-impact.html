How Coronavirus Accelerated Online Marketing and App Installs?

19 May, 2020

The Coronavirus has impacted the whole world. Whether the country is developed or developing, it has created an uncertainty and fear in the entire world.

It is badly affecting the global economy and brought down even the strongest of nations.

A huge number of people are treated for this disease and millions are quarantined in their homes.

Social distancing is the only way right now as there is no medicine or vaccine available around the globe.

Schools, colleges, non-essential stores and malls are closed, as millions of people are stuck in their homes with streaming apps, mobile apps and televisions for information and entertainment while IT professionals have been asked to work from home.






When it comes to app installs, this month has broken all the records. In the first week of lockdown/self-isolation, a huge increase of downloads of different apps were noticed such as
  • News apps: SmartNews and Newsbreak were very high in the first week of the self-isolation as we need to be updated about the current situation.
  • Social media apps: uncountable people are already on social media but this month, there was a great increase in the downloads of social media apps.
  • House party apps: Download of these kinds of apps took a huge hit this month in order to stay in touch with families and friends.
  • Food delivery apps: A record-breaking increase has been noticed this month in download of food delivery apps.
  • Education apps: Students are now at their homes and these apps are proved to be very helpful to keep children busy and continue their studies. As a result, download of education apps have increased by 17%.
  • Shopping apps: Downloads of shopping apps have increased by 40% over the month. People prefer online shopping for buying groceries and other essential items.
  • Remote Desktop Apps: Corona pandemic has forced millions of people to work from home. Remote desktop apps work best when working remotely. It safely connects the employees to a computer at another location, so that they can work on it as if they are sitting in front of it. Use of remote desktop app have increased by 60% over the month.
  • Business Apps: During COVID19 pandemic, people are compelled to work from home. And as a result global downloads of business apps have increased 90% in comparison to 2019.

User experiences and customer behaviors have drastically changed due to the Corona pandemic and locking people up in their homes.

A survey of nearly 13,000 customers by GlobalWebIndex was conducted between 16th to 20th of March 2020. This survey found that approximately 95% of people all over the world are spending their time watching online videos, movies, television.

People have also started taking interest in watching news. This survey also found that entertainment media has changed over the last two months because of the lockdown.

Great increase has been noticed as
  • 50% of people spend most of their time on streaming apps.
  • 45% of people spend most of their time on Social media.
  • Messaging apps have also kept 45% of people busy.
  • While 10% of people are sending their time on creating and uploading videos.

So, to keep up with the changing behavior of customers, businesses are also moving to digital media for marketing and looking for more digital marketing methods.

63% of businesses are planning to change the tone of message to stay relevant to the current situation.
